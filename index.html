    0:58 — 1:30

    He pockets the phone. Walks to the mess. For the first time today, the ocean is just the ocean again. Reporting, without the report.

THE THREE INSANE THINGS

Three features that make the room go quiet.

🌊
01

Geo-Aware Project Inference

DECK never asks "which project?" It already knows. Vessel AIS + phone GPS auto-resolve project, cost center, and client — before the engineer types the first letter.

3–5% project margin recovered.
🛰️
02

Sat-Lag Mode (Offline-Native)

Beyond 12 miles, bandwidth is precious. WhatsApp queues the voice note natively. The instant a packet of bandwidth opens — even 800ms — DECK transmits, transcribes, and posts. With the original capture timestamp preserved.

Zero data loss. Compliance-grade.
🎙️
03

Mess Hall Mode

The killer trick. DECK detects when an engineer is describing yesterday in past tense, casually, mid-sentence — and silently extracts the report. No commands. No mode-switching. You just talk about your day.

Zero training needed.
